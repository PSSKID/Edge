EDGE Sales Training is Customized for YOU

We design and deliver B2B sales training programs that enable your sales team to: connect and engage more effectively with prospective buyers, craft compelling solutions, and drive a greater percentage of opportunities to closure. The EDGE program is tailored to address your companys unique goals and challenges; in addition to customizing content (eg. Role-play assignments) to make it relevant to your industry. 

How is EDGE different than other Sales Training Organizations? 

Value 

Historically, this type of sales content was only available to Fortune 500 organizations EDGEs strategy is to make it affordable and accessible to small and mid-sized companies.

Personalized

Tell me and I forget, teach me and I may remember, involve me and I learn. The EDGE program includes one-on-one coaching sessions over a 4 to 6 month period for each sales participant after the on-site training engagement. We coach your sales members through real-life examples as a way to reinforce, practice and perfect their new skills.

Customized 

As part of the engagement, we spend time with your Sales Leadership to gain a better understanding of your unique challenges and requirements. We use this information to customize the content to deliver greater value to you and your sales members.

Relevant 

Our training content is always kept fresh and up-to-date to reflect todays environment. Social Selling and Insight Selling through Value Statements are examples of essential learnings incorporated into the program.

Experience 

All of our Sales Coaches have a minimum of 7 years of Sales Coaching experience, and at least 7 years of direct B2B selling. 

Agile 

EDGE offers flexible payment packages that can adapt to any business budget. 

How is the EDGE program delivered? 

OnSite

Our instructors provide training right at your location. 

Manager Feedback Sessions

Evaluation calls with Sales Managers to review progress and identify developmental areas of opportunity

One-on-One

Individualized coaching provided for a period of 4 to 6 months on actual sales cycles.
